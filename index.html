Kanaan Consulting US, Inc. is a Cambridge-based engineering company that offers comprehensive engineering and consulting services with a focus on multimodal transportation. We partner with our clients to move people and cargo more safely and efficiently by applying cutting-edge solutions and innovative yield management concepts.

comprehensive engineering and consulting services

KCUS offers a unique combination of design, implementation, operations, and maintenance services to our clients. We specialize in solutions that integrate software, hardware, and usability to deliver better outcomes—whether through improved network throughput, increased yield, refined user experience, or a combination of these.

KCUS was established in 2001. We are based in historical Bulfinch Square in Cambridge, MA.

KCUS was awarded the contract for the Massachusetts Statewide Real Time Trafffic Management (RTTM) System.
In October KCUS moved to East Cambridge. The new office is located at 43 Thorndike Street in Bulfinch Square.
KCUS offered technical assistance to the Colombian Ministry of Transport regarding the implementation of Intelligent Transportation System (ITS) technologies.
