Integrated Transportation Management System (ITMS)

Client MassDOT    Location Massachusetts    Duration 2015 - 2020      

KCUS is assisting the Massachusetts Department of Transportation (MassDOT) in the detailed design and implementation process for a new Integrated Transportation Management System (ITMS) to be deployed at MassDOT's Highway Operations Center (HOC). This software is being developed based on the Advanced Traffic Management System (ATMS)/Advanced Traveler Information System (ATIS) and Integrated Project Control System (IPCS)/Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA) design documentation that was produced by KCUS. KCUS is providing technical support and design services to MassDOT and is working with the selected ITMS vendor throughout the detailed design and implementation of the ITMS. KCUS will also assist MassDOT in the testing, verification, and validation of the new ITMS platform.
As part of this project, KCUS is also collaborating with MassDOT Legal to develop a contract that details the terms and conditions, the format of the acquisition, and the phasing of the project. This process will lead to the signing of the final contract for the acquisition and phased deployment of the ITMS platform.
