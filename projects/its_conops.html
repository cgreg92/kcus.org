I-495 ITS Concept of Operations

Client MassDOT    Location Masachusetts    Duration 2012 - 2013   

KCUS developed the ITS Concept of Operations (ConOps) and System Requirements Specification (SyRS) for the I-495 ITS deployment.
KCUS conducted interviews and meetings with the projects’ stakeholders to gather critical information on the systems’ existing condition, objectives and priorities, and operational needs. The documents adhered to the FHWA Systems Engineering Process.
The project also included the development of ConOps and SyRS templates that can be used by MassDOT for the implementation of similar systems in the future. The templates provide customizable outlines for the three types of systems, including a list of common considerations and MassDOT standards.
