Emily Wright
Engineer
Ms. Wright is a Transportation Systems Engineer specializing in Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS). She has experience in the design and development of ITS-related software applications and systems. Ms. Wright worked as part of a team to provide technical assistance for the implementation of ITS technologies to Colombia's Ministry of Transport. Ms. Wright's experience includes projects involving design, prototyping, marketing, project management and control systems.
Ms. Wright has a Bachelor of Science degree in Mechanical Engineering and a minor in Engineering Management from Tufts University.
