Regional ITS Architecture Updates

Client MassDOT    Location Massachusetts    Duration 2010   

KCUS reviewed and updated the four regional ITS architectures to reflect the changes in the National ITS Architecture, the reorganization of transportation agencies in Massachusetts, the updated Regional Transportation Plans (RTPs), and new transportation projects, plans, policies, procedures, and infrastructure implemented after 2005. These federally-mandated architectures established a framework for interagency ITS coordination for four separate regions covering the entire state. These regions were: Western Massachusetts, Central Massachusetts, Metropolitan Boston, and Southeastern Massachusetts.
